Primitive Types in Rust

Apr 08, 2020

Tips¶

  • Integer types:
    • u8 (0 to 255),
    • i8 (-128 to 127),
    • u16 (0 to 65,535),
    • i16 (-32,768 to 32,767),
    • u32 (0 to 4,294,967,295),
    • i32 (-2,147,483,648 to 2,147,483,647),
    • u64,
    • i64.
      Notice that i32 is the default type for integers.
  • Float types: f32, f64 (default)
  • Boolean: bool
  • Character: char
  • Tuple
